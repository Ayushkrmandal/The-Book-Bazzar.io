Featured Books

A Gentleman in Moscow

Author: Amor Towles

Genre: Historical Fiction

Description: The story of Count Alexander Rostov, who is sentenced to house arrest in a luxury hotel in Moscow, exploring his life and relationships over several decades.

Life of Pi

Author: Yann Martel

Genre: Adventure/Fiction

Description: The story of Pi Patel, a boy stranded on a lifeboat in the Pacific Ocean with a Bengal tiger, exploring themes of faith, survival, and the human spirit.

The Road

Author: Cormac McCarthy

Genre: Post-Apocalyptic Fiction

Description: A haunting tale of a father and son journeying through a desolate America after an unspecified disaster, struggling to survive while maintaining their humanity.

The Secret Garden

Author: Frances Hodgson Burnett

Genre: Children's Fiction

Description: This classic children's novel tells the story of Mary Lennox, a lonely girl who discovers a hidden garden that transforms her life and the lives of those around her.

The Chronicles of Narnia: The Lion, the Witch and the Wardrobe

Author: C.S. Lewis

Genre: Fantasy

Description: Four siblings enter the magical land of Narnia through a wardrobe, where they join the battle against the White Witch with the help of Aslan, a noble lion.

The Fault in Our Stars

Author: John Green

Genre: Young Adult Fiction

Description: A touching story about two teenagers, Hazel and Gus, who meet in a cancer support group and develop a deep bond while facing the realities of their illnesses.

Fictional Books

The Seven Husbands of Evelyn Hugo

Author: Taylor Jenkins Reid

Description: A reclusive Hollywood icon, Evelyn Hugo, recounts her glamorous yet tumultuous life to a young journalist, revealing her seven marriages, her rise to fame, and her quest for love and identity.

Anxious People

Author: Fredrik Backman

Description: A failed bank robber takes a group of strangers hostage during an open house. The story explores the hostages’ interconnected lives, their anxieties, and the unexpected bonds that form under pressure.

Pachinko

Author: Min Jin Lee

Description: A failed bank robber takes a group of strangers hostage during an open house. The story explores the hostages’ interconnected lives, their anxieties, and the unexpected bonds that form under pressure.

The Vanishing Half

Author: Brit Bennett

Description: This novel tells the story of twin sisters, Stella and Desiree, who choose different paths—one lives as a Black woman, while the other passes for white. Their lives diverge, yet their past continues to influence their futures.

The Midnight Library

Author: Matt Haig

Description: Nora Seed finds herself in a library between life and death, where she can explore alternate versions of her life based on choices she could have made. Each book she opens leads her to a different reality.

Eleanor Oliphant Is Completely Fine

Author: Gail Honeyman

Description: Eleanor, a socially awkward woman with a strict routine, begins to break out of her shell after developing feelings for a musician. The story highlights her journey of healing and self-discovery.

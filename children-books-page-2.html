Children's Books

The Tale of Peter Rabbit

Author: Beatrix Potter

Description: The adventurous Peter Rabbit sneaks into Mr. McGregor’s garden and finds himself in all sorts of trouble.

Charlotte's Web

Author: E.B. White

Description: A story of friendship between a pig, Wilbur, and Charlotte, a spider who saves him by weaving words into her web.

Harry Potter and the Sorcerer’s Stone

Author: J.K. Rowling

Description: The first book in the Harry Potter series introduces Harry, who learns he is a wizard and begins his magical education at Hogwarts.

The Gruffalo

Author: Julia Donaldson

Description: A clever mouse outwits woodland predators by inventing a terrifying creature, the Gruffalo, but is surprised when he meets a real one.

Oh, the Places You’ll Go!

Author: Dr. Seuss

Description: An inspirational book that encourages children to navigate life's challenges with optimism, resilience, and courage.

The Snowy Day

Author: Ezra Jack Keats

Description: A young boy named Peter explores his neighborhood after the first snowfall, capturing the joy and wonder of a snowy day.

Children's Books

Where the Wild Things Are

Author: Maurice Sendak

Description: Max, a young boy in a wolf costume, travels to a forest inhabited by wild creatures and becomes their king.

The Very Hungry Caterpillar

Author: Eric Carle

Description: A small caterpillar eats his way through a variety of foods, growing larger until he transforms into a butterfly.

Goodnight Moon

Author: Margaret Wise Brown

Description: A gentle bedtime story where a young rabbit says goodnight to the objects in his room before falling asleep.

The Cat in the Hat

Author: Dr. Seuss

Description: Two children are visited by a mischievous cat who causes chaos, but ultimately teaches them about responsibility.

Green Eggs and Ham

Author: Dr. Seuss

Description: Sam-I-Am tries to convince a reluctant character to try green eggs and ham, encouraging children to try new things.

Matilda

Author: Roald Dahl

Description: Matilda, a young girl with extraordinary intelligence and telekinetic powers, stands up to her tyrannical headmistress.

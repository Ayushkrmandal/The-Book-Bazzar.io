Children's Books

The Rainbow Fish

Author: Marcus Pfister

Description: The Rainbow Fish is a beautiful creature with shimmering scales. When he learns to share his scales with others, he discovers the true meaning of happiness and friendship.

Winnie the Pooh

Author: A.A. Milne

Description: The adventures of Winnie the Pooh and his friends in the Hundred Acre Wood are filled with wisdom and whimsy.

Brown Bear, Brown Bear, What Do You See?

Author: Bill Martin Jr. & Eric Carle

Description: This repetitive and rhythmic story takes children through a series of animals, each described in vivid colors. It helps young readers develop pattern recognition and visual learning skills.

Guess How Much I Love You

Author: Sam McBratney

Description: Little Nutbrown Hare and Big Nutbrown Hare express their love for each other in increasingly imaginative and affectionate ways, showcasing the bond between parent and child.

The Giving Tree

Author: Shel Silverstein

Description: This emotional story tells the relationship between a boy and a tree. As the boy grows older, he continues to take from the tree until nothing is left, revealing themes of generosity, sacrifice, and unconditional love.

Corduroy

Author: Don Freeman

Description: A teddy bear named Corduroy, who lives in a department store, embarks on a nighttime adventure to find his missing button in the hopes of being bought by a child. The story reflects themes of belonging and friendship.

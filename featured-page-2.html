Featured Books

The Alchemist

Author: Paulo Coelho

Genre: Adventure/Philosophical Fiction

Description: This allegorical novel follows Santiago, a shepherd boy, on his journey to discover his personal legend and fulfill his dreams.

Fahrenheit 451

Author: Ray Bradbury

Genre: Dystopian Fiction

Description: In a future where books are banned and "firemen" burn them, the story follows Montag, a fireman who begins to question society's anti-book stance.

The Hobbit

Author: J.R.R. Tolkien

Genre: Fantasy

Description: A prelude to the "Lord of the Rings," this novel follows the adventures of Bilbo Baggins, who embarks on a quest to help a group of dwarves reclaim their treasure.

The Kite Runner

Author: Khaled Hosseini

Genre: Historical Fiction

Description: A poignant story of friendship and redemption set against the backdrop of Afghanistan's tumultuous history, centered on the relationship between Amir and Hassan.

The Picture of Dorian Gray

Author: Oscar Wilde

Genre: Gothic Fiction

Description: This novel explores themes of vanity and moral duplicity through the life of Dorian Gray, whose portrait ages while he remains youthful.

The Da Vinci Code

Author: Dan Brown

Genre: Thriller/Mystery

Description: A gripping tale of art, history, and religion, this novel follows symbologist Robert Langdon as he uncovers secrets hidden in famous works of art.
